Neha Sharma

{{ site.academic_title }}
{{ site.affiliation }}

I study pricing, incentives, information, and governance design in digital marketplaces, online communities, and access-oriented markets. My work combines empirical analysis, stochastic models, and game theory to understand how platform rules shape participation, service quality, and welfare.

Current projects examine online communities, human-AI collaboration, EV access and asset-backed lending, supply constraints in sharing platforms, and how LLMs are changing technical knowledge-sharing networks.
