Hello there, and welcome to my homepage!

I am an Assistant Professor of Operations, Information, and Decisions at The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania. My research focuses on the design of online marketplaces using data, stochastic models, and game theory. I am passionate about solving problems to improve access to EVs for low-income individuals, sharing assets in emerging markets, and incentivizing consumers to reduce peak grid loads. I am also interested in understanding how LLMs are impacting knowledge-sharing networks. My research has been recognized and selected as a finalist for the IBM Best Student Paper, the IBM Service Science Best Cluster Paper, and I won the best student presentation in INFORMS. Previously, I completed my PhD in Operations Research at The Kellogg School, Northwestern University in 2023. I also teach an undergraduate elective, OIDD 2200: Operations Management Analytics in Spring.
